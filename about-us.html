About Us

IB Resume was founded by a team of current and previous elite Investment Banking professionals with a common goal of helping professionals navigate their way through the traditional Investment Bank and Buy-Side recruiting processes. The KEY to recruiting in the financial industry is YOUR resume. Your resume is everything. It’s your story, identity, a brag sheet, an elevator pitch, and more. Investment Banking recruiters and seniors have been trained to screen resumes for certain items. They spend on average 30 seconds looking at a first when first received, so every little detail relating to wording and structure counts. Through our team of professionals and structured review process, we’re confident that we know exactly how to craft a resume to make it past the application processes and initial screens.

Our team of professionals have successfully navigated their ways into Investment Banking from both the traditional college graduate path as well as non-finance routes. A majority of our team members have then successfully transitioned to buy-side roles at some of the most reputable mega funds in the private equity and hedge-fund space. The first step at getting the initial interviews? A well-crafted and structured resume.

Through our one-on-one review process, we use our years of experience and knowledge to help our customers craft a perfect resume tailored towards their recruiting needs. Satisfaction and success rates are our top priorities; if you are unhappy with the service or don’t find yourself getting interviews, contact us and we will be glad to assist.
